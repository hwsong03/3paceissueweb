What is Wildlife Trafficking?

Wildlife trafficking is a major threat to biodiversity, impacting countless species globally. It not only affects animals but also destabilizes ecosystems and fuels organized crime.

Quick Statistics:

  • Over 20,000 African elephants are poached each year.
  • Wildlife trafficking is worth over $10 billion annually.
  • Over 100 tigers are trafficked every year.
